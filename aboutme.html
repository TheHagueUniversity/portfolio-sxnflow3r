ABOUT ME

Hey! I’m Jamie, an aspiring UX Designer and full time student based in The Hague, Netherlands. I’m passionate about creating practical and aesthetic experiences for our world.
I’ve recently graduated from an American International School in Hong Kong. I have lived in Hong Kong for my whole life and am currently a first year user experience design student at The Hague University of Applied Sciences.

For past experiences, I worked as a personal assistant for a startup brand, providing my assistance to both administrative and marketing duties. It was during my time at school and my job that I learnt how important stories are to people. A story and purpose behind a design is what grasp a user’s attention. With that, it started my interest in UX/UI Design.

In my down time, you’ll either find me in yoga studio, thinking about travelling, or with a cup of coffee in my hand.
